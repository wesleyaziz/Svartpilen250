The Urban Explorer

The Svartpilen 250 dominates urban streets with its powerful engine and lightweight chassis, offering agile handling for an exciting city ride. Its upright riding position provides confidence and a clear view of the road. The motorcycle’s distinctive dark design reflects the Swedish heritage of the brand.

Chassis Details

A unique appearance is created through the authentic mix of both classic and modern design. Lightweight and narrow bodywork curves tightly around the chassis enabling seamless rider movements while the machine's dark demeanour, rugged tyres, and protective parts like the flyscreen and skid plate ensure the Svartpilen 250 has an undeniably distinctive look.
